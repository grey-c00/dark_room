Design the high level of a delivery system

1. Functional Requirement
    - The system should allow users to place orders for food delivery.
    - Users should be able to track their orders in real-time.
    - The system should provide a user-friendly interface for both customers and delivery partners.

 2. Non-Functional Requirement
    - The system should be scalable to handle a large number of users and orders.
    - Single person should not be delivering more than 20 orders at a time.
    - Higher rated delivery partners should be prioritized for order assignments.
